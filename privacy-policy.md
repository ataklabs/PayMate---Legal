# PayMate Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## 1. Introduction

PayMate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address and display name when you create an account
- **Work Data**: Hours worked, earnings, job profiles, and related calculations you enter
- **Payment Information**: Handled securely by Google Play; we do not store payment details

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (type, operating system)
- App usage analytics
- Crash reports

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the PayMate service
- Sync your data across devices
- Process subscription payments (via Google Play)
- Improve our app and user experience
- Send important service updates

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
- All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
- We implement industry-standard security measures

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Google Firebase**: For data storage and authentication
- **RevenueCat**: For subscription management
- **Google Play**: For payment processing

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your data

To exercise these rights, use the Settings menu in the app or contact us.

## 7. Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days

## 8. Children's Privacy

PayMate is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

## 10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions:
- Email: paymate.app.au@gmail.com

## 11. Australian Privacy Act

This app complies with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).
